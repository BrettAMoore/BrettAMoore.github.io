Session 3

May 24th, 2021

Capricornus 5th, 159

Having prepared for their journey, the Heirs departed Holwick. Kirri Starnon, Kruxx, and Zugash watched their progeny depart. A tall figure that might have been Aedin Tarathiel observed from a further distance.

Capricornus 6th, 159

The Heirs reached the Black Swamp's edge. They spotted a one-legged farmer and waved at him, he waved back. The Heirs walked over, and struck up a conversation. The man, Kostas Koufos, relayed that he'd lived on the farm all his life. In the last few days, the swamp had advanced about twenty feet. He'd lost his leg, wife, and children during the Dark Times, and was enslaved by the Invaders, growing food for them. He was the fifth generation of the Koufos to work the farm, and would be the last, as his children were dead. The Heirs planned to find a surrogate heir for him in Holwick when they returned.

One day, back in 134, he'd seen eight impressive-looking figures enter the Black Swamp. Six were the Six Great Heroes, the other two were a tall armored woman with religious symbols, and a stout man in sackcloth robes with a bowl haircut.

Koufos invited the Heirs in for stew, and played the jug, accompanying Lanaster on the cittern. He told them it was the best day he'd had in twenty years.

In time, the Heirs had to move along, and enter the Black Swamp.

Capricornus 7th, 159

The Heirs traveled through the Black Swamp. It was putrescent place, dark, dense, and sopping wet. Edible food and potable water was difficult to find, the organic matter was decayed and the water was foul.

Capricornus 8th, 159

The Heirs made camp, and set down for bed. During Delekhan's watch, he heard something moving out in the muck. He roused Lanaster, and the others in turn, then cast obscuring mist to cloak the campsite.

From out of the fog came four muck-men, who launched an all-out attack on the Heirs. They landed strong blows, but the Heirs rallied and fought well as a team, killing two muckmen, putting a third into magical sleep, and sending the fourth into a desperate plea, that the Heirs had conquered them and now commanded them.

The Heirs heard the muckman out. He gave his name as Wrstr, a Bagiennik from the M'hok tribe. A few months before, their tribe had been attacked and conquered by a sinister band, led by something named Kardur. Kardur had elevated the lowest caste of the tribe into the highest position, and condemed the high caste to slavery, patrolling the swamp to stop anyone moving east to west, and denying them access to the shrine to the Great Swamp Mother.

Wrstr was allowed to wake Nu'wur, who elaborated. Centuries before, the first and greatest chieftain, Y'ond, had united the scattered Bagienniks of the Black Swamp into a tribe. He divided them into three castes, low, middle, and high. Each passed its status to any new Bagienniks who budded from them. The low caste was forbidden to live in the village, or to visit the shrine.

The Heirs offered to depose Kardur. They were divided as to whether to restore the old caste system (Delekhan), topple it (Kogrash), or leave it to the Bagiennik culture to resolve (Kyruss).

Nu'wur agreed to lead them to the M'hok village. If they defeated Karstur and restored Nu'wur as chief, he would give them safe passage through the M'hok lands.

Capricornus 9th-11th, 159

The Bagienniks led the Heirs through the Black Swamp, and helped them find food and potable water.

Capricornus 12th, 159

The group reached the edge of the M'hok village.
