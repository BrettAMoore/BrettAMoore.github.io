The Six Great Heroes

The Six Great Heroes of Doncaster traveled to the west in the year 134, where they destroyed Sarkhan Vol and ended the Dark Times.

The Six are:

Aedin Tarathiel, Foremost of Elvandar
John Cooper, the Trees' Eyes
Kirri Starnon, Hell's Mistress
Kruxx, Chief of Peace
Myles Mornshield, the Lightbringer of Doncaster
Zugash, Aegis of Holwick
