Session 16

November 1st, 2021 (No Matt)

Pisces 19th, 159

Damemon, the sole survivor, walked south to the forest. Four hours later, Delekhan and Kyruss awoke. Delekhan made a stretcher for Bridgette's body, and they made camp by the barn.

Pisces 20th, 159

Damemon walked toward Hehil.

A lone rider arrived at the barn, a redheaded woman. Jolene the news courier, took their report and traveled further east toward Yisan. Kyruss cast divination, learning that he could find Damemon in Hehil.

In Hehil, Damemon saw a protest group outside the Church of Fate, demanding Yisan. They greeted Damemon warmly, and he worked with a bard to compose a ballad. He was gifted a room at the Hog's Snout. That night, the alarm bell rang, and the townsfolk barricaded themselves, terrified of the bone monster. They told Damemon that the night after the monster was driven off by the protector, the protector's servant came around to collect the tribute of silver. They worried about their ability to keep making the payments.

Damemon ran out, and saw a large monster made of bones, with mandibles made of sharpened human femurs. Damemon struck it with a chromatic orb, which sailed through the creature and damaged a house. He realized that it was an illusion, and dispelled it. An old woman emerged, and spread the news. A hunky angel appeared with a flaming sword, saw that the bone monster was gone, and flew away, promising to send its assistant the next night to gather tribute.

Pisces 21st, 159

Winds whipped through the gray wasteland, abrading exposed skin. Kyruss and Delekhan reached Hehil, and heard a new ballad about Damemon's triumph over the bone monster, and that he was holding court at the Hog's Snout. They went to the Church, and were let in and asked to leave the bodies there. Met with Priscilla the priestess. She told Delekhan of the Pumpkin King, who was said to have great power over life and growing things. She also agreed to guard the bodies in exchange for a favor.

The pair entered the Hog's Snout, where Damemon was singing. The surviving Heirs reunited. They learned that the protector's assistant would come to the northwest corner of town to begin collecting tribute. As the sun set, a black cloaked figure appeared, collecting the scant tributes, then flying off. Delekhan's owl reported back with a location. They followed the owl to a cornfield, which was still standing tall despite the season. They saw the dau, and the Pumpkin King appeared.

Bargain struck. Retrieve scepter of the hierophant of the old Church of Flesh. Lanaster and Bridgette planted.

Pisces 22nd, 159

At dawn, Lanaster and Bridgette retrieved from soil, alive but part plant. They would live until sunset.

The Heirs reunited in Hehil. Lanaster was out of sorts, and went to a dwarven smithy, where he traded his rapier and shield for two shortswords. The Heirs visited Priscilla, who struck a deal - they could enter the extradimensional space that held the Hierophant, in order to get the Pumpkin King out of her hair.

Altar moved aside, stairwell revealed. One hundred feet down, magical barrier.

Another hundred feet, then chamber w inlaid nervous system and cadavers on slabs. Quoreq fisting a Malleable.

Plasticized body room, giant husk and flesh reaver.
