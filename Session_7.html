Session 7

July 5th, 2021 (No Rob or Brady)

Capricornus 25th, 159 continued

The Heirs swapped their guard shift, bringing Damemon and Delekhan into the abbey and posting Kyruss and Lanaster on guard. Delekhan stayed upstairs reading Hazduhr's journals, while the others went to open the final unexplored door.

The door was opened, revealing a dimly lit chamber, containing a larger shrine to Sarkhan Vol, a larger and more elaborate altar, and larger pews. An ogre knelt before the altar, gorging itself on meat and baked goods. Supervising from the other side of the altar, urging the ogre on, was a grossly bloated undead monstrosity with gray skin, a distended belly, claws, and red eyes. It wore clerical vestments of exquisite quality.

The undead objected to the intrusion, and battle began. When informed that Hazduhr had been killed, the undead - named Bluk - was pleased, as it now commanded the abbey, and would pledge itself to Sarkhan Vol and rule as Bluk, Abbot of Austerity. It found Hazduhr's policies to be too lenient, and pledged a harsher regime. The ogre was swiftly cut down, and Bluk ate its carcass to heal itself.

The Heirs learned the hard way that Bluk emitted noxious gas when stabbed, while was more than Kogrash could bear, sending him to the marble floor, dying. The remaining Heirs rallied, sending Bridgette to stabilize Kogrash while Delekhan got into close range with Bluk and Damemon landed a fireball. Delekhan was mauled, and chose to withdraw to safety. Damemon was left to face the fury of Bluk, who bit him. Damemon responded with a hellish rebuke, whose merciless flames finally toppled the bloated ghoul.

The Heirs barracaded themselves inside the abbey to rest, guarding the fallen Kogrash.

Capricornus 26th, 159

Light winds. The intense heat finally broke.

A boatright knocked on the door of the abbey, nervously reporting that a boat with a damaged hull had been repaired.

Bridgette ordered the residents of the outbuildings away, gave them money, and burned their buildings. Two crates, each containing 250 pounds of gold, were placed in a river boat. The abbey was then burned, and the second floor collapsed on the first.

The Heirs debated their course of action. They boarded the boat and followed the Tiver River.

Capricornus 27th, 159

Light winds

The Heirs reached the intersection of the Tiver and Tamar Rivers. They landed on the bank, hid their boat and gold, and began marching north, along the east bank of the Tamar, through a swamp.

Capriconus 28th, 159

The Heirs marched north along the eastern bank of the Tamar, through a swamp. Bridgette, a swamp expert, led the way.

Capricornus 29th, 159

The Heris reached the grasslands north of the swamp.

Capricornus 30th, 159

The Heirs advanced north along the river.

Aquarius 1st, 159

Light winds.

The Heirs reached the town of Fleming. It had a population of about 500, and was built along the banks of the Tamar River.

Entered the Lucky Boar Inn. The inkeeper, believing them to be officers of Hazduhr, offered to give them a list of farmers who were breaking quota - he'd done so with previous officials in exchange for a reward. Bridgette menaced him, pulling him by the collar across the bar. The news was broken that the Abbey was gone, burned to ash.

Delekhan bought cold-weather clothes from a haberdasher.
