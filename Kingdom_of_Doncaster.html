Kingdom of Doncaster

The Kingdom of Doncaster is a politically-independent nation, spanning approximately 4,500 square miles, perched on the border of the unexplored Western Reaches. Its capital is Maltby.

History

Doncaster was settled in the year 0 by pioneers emigrating from the civilized east in search of arable land.

In the year 130, there came scores of deformed birds, flying in from the West. Afterwards came many ill omens and dark tidings. This culminated in an invasion from the West, by an abominable horde of monsters.

The invaders swiftly defeated the militia, and imposed degradation and slavery upon the people of Doncaster. This five-year period is known as the Dark Times.

Government

Doncaster is ruled by a king, who is elected to a life term by an assembly of landowners, and may be removed by them. The current ruler is King Harold III.

Geography

Doncaster is bordered to the west by the Black Swamp, to the north by the Blue Swamp, to the south by dense forest (which includes Elvandar), and to the east by rolling hills.

The Aire River valley is the most fertile and populous region of the kingdom, running south to north. The capital of Maltby lies on the banks of the Aire.
