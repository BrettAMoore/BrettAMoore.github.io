Mileena, the Mindrazer

Mileena, the Mindrazer is one of the Lords of Ruin.

In her portrait, Mileena was depicted as a severe-looking pale elven woman, about 60 (human equivalent), with dark hair in a bun, and sunken eyes. Her left eye is black, and she wears an uncomfortable high-collared dress.

Her domain was north and west of Hazduhr's. It contained the villages of Camden, Torbay, and Torridge.

Mileena's people were subject to these rules, enforced by huntsmen and hounds who captured and removed rulebreakers:

#1 - no religious practices
#2 - no travel unless you can reach your destination and return home before the sun sets
#3 - no written words
#4 - no speaking to travelers
#5 - no symbols, drawings, or map-making
#6 - no magic

Mileena was confronted by the Heirs in her home, a wizard's college she'd founded and then dissolved. She told her story to the Heirs, how she believed in the power of education to uplift the common man, and swore to destroy Sarkhan Vol when his forces came and shut down her schools. When Sarkhan Vol was defeated, Mileena vowed to stay in the West for ten years. She was joined by Aedin Tarathiel, who fathered a son - Delekhan - with Mileena, then abducted him and fled. Mileena stayed behind to honor her vow.

Over the years, Mileena came to believe that sharing knowledge only made it less valuable, and made her weaker. She shut down her college and converted the students into constructs. When Sarkhan Vol returned, he granted Mileena his left eye, which allowed her to know the thoughts of anyone she saw. She built a magical pillar that allowed her to monitor and edit the minds of her subjects, and imparted the rules to them. She amssaed a library, reading each book and then destroying it so no other could read it.

Mileena began work on her greatest ambition - creating "languages", which would divide people into mutually unintelligble groups, unable to access one another's knowledge. She was confronted and killed by the Heirs before she could finalize her plans.
